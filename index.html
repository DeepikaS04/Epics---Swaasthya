Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are these consultations chargeable?

    The first consultation is free of cost as well as the follow-up consultations which is valid for 3 days. A minimal fee applies from the second consultation.

  • What is the qualification of the doctors?

    The doctors belong to the top medical colleges of India and are experts in their respective fields.

  • Can I get information about vaccination centers?

    Yes, your nearest vaccination center will be shown to you according to your address.
